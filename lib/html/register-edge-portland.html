The EDGE Cyber Summit Portland is a one-day event focusing on Risk & Privacy Management. The summit will feature presentations from subject matter experts/practitioners, C-level panel, interactive/hands-on tabletop exercise, and opportunities for networking, discussion, and dialogue.

The agenda topics include:

  • Management Accepts the Risk: A Tabletop Exercise in Risk Management
  • Privacy Compliancy – GDPR and Beyond
  • A Primer on Cyber Insurance
  • Third Party Risk Management Program Development
  • Enterprise Risk Assessment – A Case Study
