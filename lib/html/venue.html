Whether you're traveling for business or pleasure, the Crowne Plaza hotel in Knoxville combines comfort with sophistication.

Crowne Plaza is located in the heart of downtown Knoxville near more than 70 restaurants and pubs, a variety of locally-owned shopping venues, world-class theaters and public parks. It is also just a few minutes walk to Market Square, the Old City, World's Fair Park and the University of Tennessee campus.

The hotel’s meeting rooms include 15,000 square feet of space in three ballrooms and features state-of-the-art technology for presentations.

The hotel features Mahogany’s, one of Knoxville’s finest restaurants; the Library, a lounge located just off the main lobby; a gift shop; business center; free Wi-Fi and complimentary cyber stations.

Conference participants staying at the hotel will enjoy the very best amenities such as a high-definition televisions, plush, firm beds, two telephones and access to an indoor heated lap pool and a state-of-the-art gym with strength and cardiovascular equipment.
