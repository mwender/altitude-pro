Personal Training

A Provision Health & Fitness, we set the highest standards of excellence when it comes to our personal training. Our trainers are fitness professionals that are educated, experienced, and skilled at designing and implementing scientifically based exercise programs.

Assessment

All of our training clients begin with a full assessment to ensure their program is just right for them! We do a detailed assessment of posture, joint mobility, body composition, and fitness level to ensure your custom exercise program addresses all of your needs and is safe.

Personal Training

Our fitness experts will design a challenging customized program that is appropriate for your fitness level, provide accountability and motivation, and give you the one-on-one attention you need to ensure every exercise is executed safely and proper.

Semi Private Training

Share an exciting fitness experience with a spouse or close friend! Our fitness experts will design a custom program that is challenging for both participants, but takes into account the unique needs of each individual.

Small Group Training

Small Group Training is a perfect blend of customization and group accountability. Our fitness experts will design a custom program that is challenging for all participants, but takes into account the unique needs of each individual. Groups range in size from 4-8 participants.
